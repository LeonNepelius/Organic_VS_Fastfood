Organic Vs Fastfood

Organic Food

  • 5 Positive Aspects
    1. Produce contains fewer pesticides
    2. Food is often fresher
    3. Farming is better for the environment
    4. Organically raised animals are NOT given antibiotics, growth hormones, or fed animal byproducts
    5. Meat and milk are richer in certain nutrients
  • 5 Negative Aspects
    1. It's more expensive to buy than non-organic food.
    2. May go ‘off’ more quickly than non-organic produce. This is because organic fruit and vegetables do not contain artificial preservatives and are not irradiated.
    3. It's not widely available
    4. It takes a lot of work
    5. Food is GMO-free (Genetically Modified Organisms)
